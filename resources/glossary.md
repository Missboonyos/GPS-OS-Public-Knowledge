# Glossary

Definitions of terms used throughout GPS OS documentation.

- **GPS OS** — GPS Operating System; the methodology documented in this repository.
- **Operational Knowledge** — Practical knowledge held by people who perform work, often undocumented.
- **Workflow** — The complete sequence of steps, people, and decisions involved in completing a task.
- **MVP** — The smallest complete solution for today's problem, not unfinished software.
- **Discover / Map / Design / Build / Validate / Ship / Learn** — The seven phases of the GPS Method. See [04_Method.md](../docs/04_Method.md).

*This glossary will grow as GPS OS is applied to more domains.*
