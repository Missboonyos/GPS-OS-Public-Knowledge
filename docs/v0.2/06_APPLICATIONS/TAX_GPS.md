# Tax GPS



# Purpose

Tax GPS demonstrates the application of GPS OS to personal and SME tax decision support.

It applies the GPS Method to transform complex tax regulations into structured operational knowledge that helps users make informed decisions with greater confidence.

The objective is not to replace professional judgment, but to improve understanding, consistency, and decision quality through reliable software.

---

# Operational Domain

Tax GPS focuses on tax planning and compliance for individuals and small businesses.

Users often need to understand tax obligations, filing requirements, deductible expenses, and financial decisions across multiple regulations and reporting periods.

Tax information is available, but it is frequently fragmented, technical, and difficult to interpret correctly.

---

# Operational Problem

Tax decisions require combining operational knowledge from multiple sources while considering changing regulations and individual business situations.

Common challenges include:

- Difficulty interpreting tax regulations
- Inconsistent financial records
- Uncertainty about filing obligations
- Repetitive manual calculations
- Risk of incorrect decisions due to misunderstanding

The problem is not the lack of information, but the lack of a structured operational decision process.

---

# GPS OS Application

Tax GPS applies the GPS Method by:

- Understanding real tax workflows
- Modeling tax-related decisions
- Structuring regulatory knowledge
- Designing explicit decision logic
- Building software that preserves operational reasoning
- Validating outputs against official regulations and operational scenarios

The product demonstrates how operational tax knowledge can be transformed into reliable decision-support software while remaining explainable and traceable.

---

# Outcomes

Tax GPS demonstrates that GPS OS can:

- Transform tax knowledge into operational software
- Improve consistency in tax-related decisions
- Reduce repetitive manual analysis
- Preserve regulatory reasoning within system design
- Support continuous learning as regulations evolve

These outcomes validate the application of GPS OS in the domain of tax and financial decision support.
