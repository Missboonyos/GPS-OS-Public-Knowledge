# Reuse Engine



# Purpose

The Reuse Engine defines how GPS OS transforms validated knowledge into reusable assets.

Its purpose is to ensure that operational learning is preserved, refined, and applied consistently across future products, projects, and decisions.

Rather than solving the same problem repeatedly, GPS OS continuously builds a growing body of reusable operational knowledge.

---

# Principle

Knowledge becomes valuable when it can be reused.

Every validated insight should strengthen future reasoning rather than remain isolated within a single project.

Reuse enables continuous improvement for both humans and AI.

---

# Reuse Process

Every reusable knowledge asset follows the same lifecycle:

Capture

↓

Validate

↓

Structure

↓

Store

↓

Discover

↓

Reuse

↓

Improve

↓

Capture

The cycle repeats as new operational experience becomes available.

---

# Reusable Knowledge Assets

Examples of reusable assets include:

- Decision Patterns
- Workflow Patterns
- Business Rules
- Design Principles
- Architecture Patterns
- Validation Results
- Case Studies
- Lessons Learned
- Specification Templates

Each asset should preserve both operational context and the reasoning that created it.

---

# Reuse Quality

A reusable knowledge asset should be:

- Validated
- Well-structured
- Discoverable
- Traceable
- Adaptable
- Independent of a specific implementation

Reuse should preserve reasoning while allowing implementation to evolve.
