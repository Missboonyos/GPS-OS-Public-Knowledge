# Roadmap



# Purpose

The Roadmap outlines the long-term evolution of GPS OS.

Rather than prescribing fixed deadlines, it communicates the intended direction of development while preserving the stability of the Canon and the flexibility of implementation.

The roadmap reflects continuous learning rather than feature accumulation.

---

# Guiding Principle

Keep the Canon stable.

Improve the implementation continuously.

Each new edition should strengthen shared understanding, operational reasoning, and knowledge reuse without compromising the core principles of GPS OS.

---

# Current Edition

Version

v0.2

Status

Core Foundation Complete

Focus

Establish the public knowledge base of GPS OS.

---

# Future Directions

Future development may include:

- Additional real-world case studies
- Expanded pattern and case libraries
- AI-assisted knowledge engineering
- Reference implementations
- Educational materials
- Community contributions
- Research publications

These initiatives extend the application of GPS OS while preserving its Canon.

---

# Long-Term Vision

GPS OS aims to become a shared operational language for humans and AI.

Its long-term objective is to enable organizations to transform operational knowledge into reliable software through transparent reasoning, reusable knowledge, and continuous learning.

---

# Roadmap Philosophy

GPS OS evolves through validated understanding.

The Canon remains stable.

Knowledge grows.

Methods improve.

Applications expand.

The operating principles remain unchanged.
